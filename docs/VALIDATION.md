# Validation Fixtures

The current astronomy checks are broad regression fixtures for the 12 August 2026 eclipse. They protect the app against obvious calculation or wiring regressions while we build the map and terrain layers.

## Fixture Groups

Totality fixtures:

- Zaragoza, Spain
- Reykjavik, Iceland
- Gijon, Spain

Partial-only fixtures:

- New York, United States
- London, United Kingdom

No visible 2026 event fixtures:

- Tokyo, Japan
- Sydney, Australia
- Buenos Aires, Argentina

## What The Tests Assert

- totality locations return a total local eclipse with ordered contacts
- totality duration stays within broad expected ranges
- partial-only locations return partial contacts without totality contacts
- no-visible locations are filtered out of the 2026 event window
- selected-time Sun/Moon positions still vary by observer coordinates

## Source Direction

The fixture categories match the published 2026 visibility pattern: totality through Greenland, Iceland, and northern Spain, with partial visibility over much of Europe and parts of North America. Exact contact times are still generated by Astronomy Engine inside the app.

Future validation should add independent fixtures from NASA/GSFC or EclipseWise Besselian data, with documented tolerances for contact times, magnitude, obscuration, altitude, and azimuth.
